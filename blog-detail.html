2026 Luxury Market Outlook: What High-Net-Worth Buyers Want

Claire Montgomery
Senior Market Analyst

Luxury demand continues to rise in coastal and lifestyle markets as high-net-worth buyers prioritize privacy, lifestyle amenities, and turnkey experiences. Inventory remains constrained, driving competitive bidding for best-in-class properties.

Key Drivers in 2026

  • Smart-home technology and sustainable design
  • Expanded outdoor entertaining and wellness zones
  • Flexible spaces for hybrid work and leisure

Investment Considerations

Buyers are increasingly favoring properties with long-term appreciation potential, stable local economies, and proximity to lifestyle hubs.

"Luxury buyers are seeking lifestyle upgrades, not just square footage."
